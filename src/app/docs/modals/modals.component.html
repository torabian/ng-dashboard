Modals and pickers in ng-dashboard

In ng-dashboard team, we provide some modals and pickers for the projects built with this framework. We are sick of broken pickers, who do not work on mobile, having accessibilty issues, and so on. Therefor, we build most of them from scratch in order to fit specific application rather than a general datepicker or timepicker solution.

(1/3) Confirm modal

We provide you a minimal modal service, to show a text and for confirmation.

(2/3) Yes/No dialog

You can change the buttons to include use and no.

(3/3) Date (day) picker

Use day picker for forms that need to select an specific day of the month. This is not a replacement for date picker, which selects specific date, or a range of dates
